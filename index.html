Rudra Mitra

Software Development Associate at 20|20 Mobile Corp

Hello! I'm Rudra, a Computer Science graduate (B.Sc., minor in Mathematics, GPA 3.73) from the University of Bridgeport, currently working as a Software Development Associate at 20|20 Mobile Corp, where I build backend services, APIs, and distributed systems for the Team Hub platform.

My research background spans NASA-funded high-altitude ballooning missions, radiation sensor development, and deep learning for wildfire monitoring. I spent time as a Lab Research Assistant at the Extreme Environment Engineering Lab, contributing to the Connecticut Space Grant program, and led STEM outreach through the university's STEM Bus.

I've co-authored several papers presented at ASEE Northeast Section Conferences, on topics ranging from wildfire monitoring and fire detection systems to interpretable AI and hybrid deep learning for pneumonia diagnosis. I also contributed to an autonomous navigation stack for the IGVC 2025 competition and served as President of the Math Club and Treasurer of IEEE and Upsilon Pi Epsilon.
