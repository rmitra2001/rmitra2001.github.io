About Me

I’m a Computer Science graduate from the University of Bridgeport with a minor in Mathematics (GPA 3.73). My work blends software engineering, AI research, robotics, and engineering systems designed for extreme environments.

I’ve contributed to NASA-funded research, developed radiation sensors for high-altitude missions, built autonomous navigation systems, and published multiple papers in ASEE conferences and engineering journals.

I enjoy building systems that solve real-world problems — from wildfire prediction models to robotic payloads and microservice architectures.
